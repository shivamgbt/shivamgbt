# 👋 About Me

I started my career with an engineering and automation background, which gave me a strong foundation in how systems, controls, and processes work in the real world. Over time, I became interested in the software and data side of technology, which led me into AI data quality and technical workflows.

I previously worked as a QA Specialist supporting AI projects, where I was involved in validating datasets, improving quality processes, and collaborating across computer vision, NLP, and audio domains. During that time, I also built tools and projects using Python and modern AI frameworks, which strengthened my interest in applied technology and problem-solving.

Right now, I’m continuing to build my skills and explore opportunities where I can contribute, learn, and grow in a hands-on technical environment.
